PHOTO MONTAGE

Blending into the Elements

2022 | Digital Imaging

As I came up with an idea for a photo composition, I thought of different sceneries I could use together, then I went on a little adventure to take some shots. Along with a little cutting, trimming, effects and inspiration from books like “Where's Waldo?” I found a way to disguise myself into the water, bushes, mountains and combine everything all into one. The concept for this composition came from my interest in collage which became a side hobby of mine as well as my interests in art styles like surrealism.
