This is Criminal Podcast

2021 | BOOK COVER DESIGN

“This is Criminal” is a podcast based on real-life crime events and hosted by American journalist, Pheobe Judge. After listening to a few episodes, I chose the three that took my interest: The Checklist, American Dream and Willie Bosket, and designed book covers based on those episodes.

HONORABLE MENTIONS | WILLIE BOSKET

My Challenge

Listening to each podcasts and grasping the "concept" from each story was difficult. To then visualize each concept and turn it into a book cover was the biggest challenge for me.

My Solution

Sketching different variations of the concept helped me visualize and gave me the direction I needed to create the book cover.
